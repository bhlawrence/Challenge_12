Belly Button Biodiversity Dashboard

Use the interactive charts below to explore the dataset

This website contains data from a study conducted to discover what bacterial species exist in belly buttons. Belly button swabs were obtained from a variety of people of varying hygiene regiments. The swabs were cultured and colonies isolated to determine what bacterial species may lie within. 16S ribosomal RNA sequencing was used to determine the species which are listed by their Operational Taxonomic Unit (OTU). Some species with OTU numbers are currently relatively unstudied, but may make up a significant portion of the bacteria found in a given test subjects belly button!
